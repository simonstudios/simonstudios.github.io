What's it about?

When it comes to helping others, small acts can make a big difference. Sometimes we just need a nudge. And as we help others, we discover we also help ourselves - doing good feels good!

Ripple is full of managable, accessible suggestions to get you started. Add your own suggestions and share them with the Ripple community. Track your own progress, and see the total impact too.

As we each do our bit, we create a Ripple effect. Let's spread some joy!

How do I use it?

Today

The today view is where you can select a particular ripple

Stats

See how many Ripples you've completed - and the worldwide total too!

Library

Manage your library - browse, filter, search, add, delete and edit your Ripples. Your library is available on all your devices.

New Ripple

Add new Ripples to your library. Share your ideas with the whole Ripple community.

Widget

Add the Ripple widget to your homescreen to see your active Ripple at a glance.
